Enforce the following financial rules/treatments:
For loan disbursements:
- The amount shows as debit in the external bank account.
- The transaction gets imported to the master bank account as a debit.
- The transaction gets assigned to a user loan account.
- The user's fund account is debited
- The master fund account is also debited.
For loan repayment:
- The amount shows as credit in the external bank account.
- The transaction gets imported to the master bank account as a credit.
- The transaction gets assigned to a user loan account.
- The user's bank account is credited.
- The user's bank account is then debited.
- The user's loan account is then credited.
- The user's fund account is also credited.
- The master fund account is also credited.
For Contribution:
- The amount shows as credit in the external bank account.
- The transaction gets imported to the master bank account as a credit.
- The transaction gets assigned to a user.
- The user's bank account is credited.
- The user's bank account is then debited.
- The user's fund account is then credited.
- The master fund account is also credited.
